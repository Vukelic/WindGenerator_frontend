How to Start Investing in Wind Turbines

Investing in wind turbines is an easier process than many think.There are three primary steps that you should take to begin your wind farm investments.
Wind turbines, better known as windmills, have been used for 1000 years. It is characteristic of them that they convert the energy of wind movement into rotational movement energy, so the immediate result of their function is a rotating shaft that can be driven by some working device. The efficiency coefficient of these machines (the quotient of the energy produced on the rotating shaft and the energy used by the wind for rotation) cannot exceed Betz's theoretical limit of 59.3% (a gasoline engine has about 35%). According to the mutual position of the axis of rotation of the rotor and the direction of the wind that drives it, wind turbines are divided into axial (the axis of the rotor is parallel to the ground) and radial (the axis of the rotor is perpendicular to the ground).

Our company has selected both types of turbines available to customers and they generate an average of 15,000 kilowatts per year at a wind speed of 8 m/s. The minimum wind speed to start the turbine is 2 m/s to 3 m/s which is somewhere around 7 km/h (imperceptible air flow). Those results were obtained at a height of 10 meters, which is the average height of houses in a row. What a wind generator can generate depends on the amount of wind, where it is placed and at what height it will be installed.
